## Connecting your wallet

Click the **wallet button** in the top-right corner of the header to open the connection modal. Any browser wallet works — MetaMask, Rabby, or WalletConnect-compatible wallets.

Once connected, your truncated address appears in the header (e.g., `0x1234...abcd`).

![Home page](/images/01-home-page.png)

## Browsing claims

The home page lists all claims. Each card shows:

- Claim message
- Chain badge
- Token name and symbol
- Counterparty address
- Constraints (amount, count, time range — if set)
- Proof count
- Creation date

### Filtering and sorting

- **Search** — filter by message, address, or hash
- **Chain filter** — show claims for one chain
- **Sort** — Newest First, Oldest First, Most Proofs, Least Proofs

Click **"View Details"** to open a claim — view its transfers, generate a proof, or browse submitted proofs.
