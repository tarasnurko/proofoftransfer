## ETHKyiv Impulse: ZK Edition

Proof of Transfer was born at **ETHKyiv Impulse: ZK Edition** — a hackathon organized by [Ethereum Ukraine](https://kyivethereum.com/) on December 13, 2025 in Kyiv, Ukraine.

The event focused on zero-knowledge technologies and brought together builders from the Ukrainian Ethereum community — the perfect environment to turn the idea of privacy-preserving transfer proofs into a working prototype.

## Thank you

Thank you to the **Ethereum Ukraine** team for organizing this event and creating a space where projects like this can start.

If you're interested in the Ukrainian Ethereum community or future events, check out [Ethereum Ukraine](https://kyivethereum.com/).
